About

Ali Riola is a visual, interactive graphic designer and photographer with a Bachelors in Fine Art from Cal State Fullerton and over sixteen years of professional experience. Involved in developing brand identities, textile design and motion graphics as well as various types of print collateral.

My graphic work is influenced by typography and photography. Also, having a background in photography has contributed to my graphic design by use of color application and composition. I enjoy graphic design the most when I can add my photos to my work which I feel shows the character of a designer and helps convey the message more personally.
